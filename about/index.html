About Us

We are an independent, family run business, built for people who seek the thrill of opening packs and sealed trading card products, collecting cards and for those who are sick of seeing the words "out of stock" everywhere.

Who we are...

One Two TCG is an independent online store focused on trading card games, with a particular focus (as of 2026) on Pokémon sealed products. Our primary goal is to provide a one-stop-shop to find stock in a market where availability can be unpredictable.

Why we started...

The trading card market has become increasingly difficult to navigate, with stock shortages, inflated prices, and constant sell-outs. One Two TCG was created to offer a more reliable place to find products at competitive market prices and get them dispatched to your door quick sharp.

What matters to us...

We focus on transparency, fair pricing, and making sure products are actually available when you need them. In this market we cannot make unrealistic promises — just a straightforward, no nonsense approach to getting genuine trading card products in your hands.

What you can expect...

You can expect clear and defined product listings, secure packaging with care and attention given to what your hard earned money has paid for. We'll also provide consistent communication from order to delivery and always be on hand with a professional touch to any queries you may have. As the store grows and our access to distributors expands, we’ll continue expanding our product range to include more sealed products and even other trading card games.
