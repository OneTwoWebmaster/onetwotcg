Returns

We want you to be happy with your order, however in the event of unforeseen circumstances or a problem, please see below our returns policy.

Returns Window

You can request a return within 14 days of receiving your order. Please contact us with your order number and the reason for the return before sending anything back. We would also ask that you read the below before contacting us.

Condition Requirements

Returned items must be in their original condition. Sealed products must remain unopened and undamaged to qualify for a return. All products are uniquely identified so what we send you must the be the exact same product you send back.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or you receive the wrong item, please contact us as soon as possible. We may ask for photos so we can review the issue and resolve it quickly.

Refunds

Once the returned item has been received and checked, any approved refund will be sent back to your original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
